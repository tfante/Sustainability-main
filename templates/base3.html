Purpose. Why bother?.

Over the last decade The University of Puget Sound has been working diligently with students, staff, and organizations to become a sustainable, net-zero carbon institution and progress into a leading institution for climate action.The University of Puget Sound consumes ___ of Water, Gas, electricity, on average every year. This website is intended to provide the community with the information and understanding necessary to attain our environmental goals and to think daily about your environmental impact.

Goals. What can be accomplished?

Our goal is to become a completely sustainable campus, achieving Carbon Net-zero emissions by 2030. Understanding our consumption habits and behaviors are a stepping stone into making significant and identifiable action towards this goal. Limiting individual consumption, and developing sustainable behaviors around campus is essential for our institution and our environment.

Methods. How did you measure? How can it improve?

Water, electricity, and Natural gas data collection and running analysis through python programs. Statistical modeling, linear regressions, kmeans algorithms, and machine learning modeling are some of the tests that were preformed to obtain the necessary information for this website.
